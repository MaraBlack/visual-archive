Hello there, I'm Maria

web developer & illustrator

Information

I’ve always been drawn to visual expression. Most of my work begins with simple sketches—moments, shapes, or ideas that capture my attention. I often take inspiration from artists I admire or illustrations I discover online, recreating them in my own way and exploring how small changes in line, texture, or color can shift the mood of a piece.

For me, drawing is a quiet form of observation. It’s a way of learning, studying details, experimenting with styles, and understanding how others approach visual storytelling. I enjoy browsing creative platforms, discovering new artists, and letting those influences guide my own interpretations.

Alongside my visual work, I also have a background in web development. Though the two fields are different, they both shape how I think about structure, clarity, and aesthetics. Together, they help me approach each drawing with both curiosity and intention.
